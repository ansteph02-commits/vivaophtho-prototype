Examiner · Investigations

Fundus confirms a superotemporal BRVO with macular oedema. He asks what you’ll do next. Which investigation drives your management — and why?

Your answer

OCT to quantify the macular oedema and guide anti-VEGF, plus wide-field imaging to assess the ischaemic index…

Reveal model answer ↓
Model answer · examiner voice

OCT is the immediate driver — it confirms and quantifies macular oedema, the cause of vision loss, and sets the anti-VEGF baseline. Wide-field FFA then stratifies ischaemia and neovascular risk. The curveball: what changes if he’s on anticoagulants, or if the disc looks swollen?
